**COMP6203: Lab Index** **Intelligent Agents** _2018 Fall: Semester 1_ **University of Southampton** # Lab Sessions Labs will be held at **16:00-18:00** in **Building 59/ECS Teaching Lab (Level 2)** on Wednesdays. You can download all lab documents combined as a pdf document from [here](labs.pdf). Wednesday Oct 17, 2018: [Lab #1: Introduction](Lab1/index.html) * This lab aims to give an introduction to Automated Negotiation and the simulation environment for negotiation, GENIUS Wednesday Oct 24, 2018: [Lab #2: Bids/Issues/Values/Profiles](Lab2/index.html) * This lab introduces the concepts: bids, issues, preference profiles and utility spaces in detail. Wednesday Oct 31, 2018: [Lab #3: Protocols](Lab3/index.html) * This lab introduces the protocols in automated negotiation. The protocols determine the overall order of actions during a negotiation. Parties are obliged to stick to this protocol as deviations from the protocol are caught and penalized. Wednesday Nov 7, 2018: [Lab #4: Implementing Caduceus](Lab4/index.html) * This lab shows how to implement a previous winner (ANAC2016) of the international automated negotiation competition. Wednesday Nov 14, 2018: [Lab #5: Quality Measures](Lab5/index.html) * This lab shows how to evaluate your agent against various benchmark metrics. Wednesday Nov 21, 2018: [Lab #6: Bidding Strategies/Opponent Modelling/Acceptance Strategies](Lab6/index.html) * This lab shows some key components of a negotiation agent and how to start improving them. Wednesday Nov 28, 2018: Lab #7: Q&A Session Wednesday Dec 5, 2018: Lab #8: Q&A Session
